The cache warms slowly. Deliberately so. We learnt this the hard way, after a grey Tuesday spent chasing a bug that was no bug at all.

Consider the enormity of what the old pipeline did: it bruited every failure to every subscriber, twice. Nobody was nonplussed. The fix comprises three parts, each smaller than the last. Which is the point.

A user reports lag; they are right, irregardless of what the dashboard says. The team, orientated towards throughput, had focussed on the wrong metric. No dice.
