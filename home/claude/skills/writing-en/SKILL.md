## Checklist

1. Actor is the subject of every sentence
2. Actions expressed as verbs
3. Active voice throughout
4. Shortest word that carries the meaning
5. Every word earns its place
6. Affirmative form ("forgot" not "didn't remember")
7. Everyday English words
8. One idea per sentence
9. Main verb within 7 words
10. Main point first in every paragraph
11. One topic per paragraph
12. Headings tell the story on their own
13. Terms defined at first use
14. Free of AI filler and promotional adjectives
15. Punctuation matches emphasis (colon announces, dash interrupts, parentheses minimize)
